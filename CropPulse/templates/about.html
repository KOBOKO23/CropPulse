About CropPulse

CropPulse is a digital platform designed to support agricultural researchers, meteorologists, and farmers by capturing and organizing field-level agrometeorological data.

Our Mission

We strive to strengthen food security by equipping stakeholders with reliable, real-time data that drives smart agricultural decisions.

What We Do

  • Digitize agrometeorological field reports like Agro-1 to Agro-19.
  • Generate actionable insights from sowing, phenology, yield, and weather data.
  • Provide accessible dashboards for researchers and extension officers.
  • Promote precision agriculture through data-driven tools.

Why CropPulse?

Traditionally, field data was collected manually and analyzed slowly. CropPulse bridges this gap by enabling faster, smarter, and more consistent agricultural monitoring to improve food productivity and resilience.

Who We Serve

  • Field Researchers & Agricultural Observers
  • Farmers & Farmer Cooperatives
  • NGOs & Development Partners
  • Government and Academic Institutions

Together, let's digitize farming for a more secure future.
